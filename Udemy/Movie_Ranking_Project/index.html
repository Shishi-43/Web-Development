Pursuit of Happines

The Pursuit of Happyness is a 2006 American biographical drama film based on entrepreneur Chris Gardner's nearly one-year struggle with homelessness. It stars Will Smith as Gardner, Thandiwe Newton as his wife Linda, and Jaden Smith as Gardner's son Christopher. The film was directed by Gabriele Muccino and written by Steven Conrad. It was released on December 15, 2006, by Columbia Pictures.

Maverick

Maverick is a 1994 American Western comedy film directed by Richard Donner and starring Mel Gibson, Jodie Foster, and James Garner. The screenplay by William Goldman is based on the 1950s television series of the same name created by Roy Huggins. The film follows Bret Maverick (Gibson), a gambler who must raise $3000 to enter a high-stakes poker game. He is joined by Annabelle Bransford (Foster), a fellow con artist, and Marshal Zane Cooper (Garner), a lawman who is also trying to enter the game.
